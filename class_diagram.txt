note right of EmailJob
  Email sender worker.
  Originates from Notification.
  Email type is derived from Notification.type.
  Sends to recipientEmail.
end note

note right of Notification
  Exactly one recipient target:
  - User.email OR
  - TicketDelivery.email
end note

note right of Payment
  Multiple payment attempts per Order are allowed.
  At most one Payment should reach PAID state.
end note

note bottom of Ticket
  Uniqueness hints:
  - Max one TicketDelivery per Ticket.
  - Max one QRCode per Ticket.
end note

note bottom of Role
  Uniqueness hint:
  - Role.type should be unique.
end note
